Oil rice

Description of the dish. A few lines describing the recipe's background or inspiration.

Ingredients:

  • oil
  • onions
  • Long Grain Rice
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Bay Leaves
  • Curry Powder
  • Seasoning Cubes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: • Wash the rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside to drain. • Chop the onions, bell peppers, carrots, and green beans (if using). • Mince the garlic and ginger. • Dice or shred any cooked meat (chicken, beef, or fish) if you want to include protein. • Prepare the tomatoes (chop or blend) and scotch bonnet pepper (if using). • Set all ingredients aside in separate bowls for easy access during cooking.
  2. Cook the Rice (if not pre-cooked): • In a pot, bring water (or broth) to a boil, add a pinch of salt, and cook the rice according to the package instructions until it is tender and fully cooked. Once cooked, drain any excess water and set the rice aside to cool.
  3. Fry the Vegetables: • Heat 3-4 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium heat. • Add the chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened and fragrant. • Add the chopped bell peppers, carrots, and green beans (or peas). Stir fry for another 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the Tomatoes and Seasoning: • Add the chopped tomatoes (or pureed tomatoes) to the pan and stir to combine. • Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes soften and the mixture thickens into a sauce. • Add the seasoning cubes, salt, black pepper, thyme, and curry powder (if using). Stir well and allow the sauce to simmer for another 2-3 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. Add the Rice: • Add the cooked and cooled rice to the pan, one scoop at a time, stirring gently to combine with the vegetable and tomato mixture. • Add a little more oil if needed to ensure the rice gets well-coated and fried with the vegetables. • If you’re using cooked meat, add it at this point and stir well
  8. Fry the Rice: • Stir fry the rice for about 10-15 minutes on medium heat, allowing the flavors to blend and the rice to fry lightly. Continue to stir regularly to prevent it from sticking to the pan.
  9. Optional Add-ins: • Add sweet corn or any other vegetables you like, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through. • Adjust seasoning to taste by adding more salt or pepper if necessary.
  10. Final Touches: • Once the rice is fried and fully combined with the vegetables and seasonings, remove from heat. • Garnish with freshly chopped herbs like parsley or cilantro, and serve with a squeeze of lime or lemon juice for extra freshness (optional).
  11. Serve: • Serve the oil rice hot, either on its own or with fried plantains, grilled meats, or a salad. This procedure results in a delicious, well-seasoned oil rice dish that is perfect as a main or a side dish. Enjoy!
